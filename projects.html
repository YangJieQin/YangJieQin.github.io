Projects

things I've built

TwistAR: Flexible Body, Flexible Life!

An open-source Twister-inspired exergame that encourages movement using computer vision. It was developed for users who spend long hours seated or standing in the same position. The game challenges players to match on-screen prompts with body poses detected from a webcam.

Musk2Cash

A stock trading bot powered by Elon Musk's tweets. By linking social media sentiment with market trends, it explores how online influence can be transformed into investment insights. This was inspired by the viral Trump2Cash project.

Discovering Alternate Predictors for Diabetes Diagnosis

A study that proposes a novel ensemble machine learning approach to predict diabetes using indirect medical and demographic attributes, excluding direct indicators such as insulin and blood sugar/glucose levels. The ensemble model comprises Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and a Wide and Deep Neural Network; and is enhanced using Voting and Stacking techniques.
