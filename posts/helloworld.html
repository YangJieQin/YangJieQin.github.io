Why I Started This Blog

SEP. 24, 2025

Welcome to my blog! After almost of decade of wanting to create a personal blog, this blog post officially marks the beginning of my blogging journey! Most people offline know me as a man of few words, but I do, in fact, think deeply about a gamut of topics and ideas. I hope this blog can serve as a journey through my mind and also be a place for me to organize and memorialize my random thoughts.

There will be no rhyme or reason to this blog. It was simply be whatever I want to share that is on my mind. It could be an epiphany that I had while backpacking through the Enchanted Valley, or possibly a recipe for a stew that I serendipitously concocted one night. It could be a story about an utterly peculiar stranger I met while floating down the Danube, or a rant about a bad day that I had.

Thanks for reading! I'll see you soon hopefully!
